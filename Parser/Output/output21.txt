Title: lemon meringue pie with graham-cracker crust recipe by  - chow
1 1/2 cups crushed graham crackers (about 8 whole crackers)
6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
3 tablespoons granulated sugar
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
for the lemon filling:
1/3 cup cornstarch
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
4 large egg yolks
1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (from 3 to 4 lemon:lemon:lemon:lemon:lemon:lemons)
1 1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) unsalted butter, cut into pieces
2 tablespoons finely grated lemon zest (from 3 to 4 lemons)
for the meringue:
5 large egg whites, at room temperature
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 cup granulated sugar
