Tomato Cucumber Salad Submitted by: Sowmya Rated: 4 out of 5 by 42 members Prep Time: 10 Minutes Ready In: 10 Minutes Yields: 4 servings " Diced tomatoes and cucumbers, chopped onion, and a splash of lemon juice. Chill and serve to four." INGREDIENTS: 2 tomatoes, chopped 1 cucumber, peeled and diced 1 onion, chopped 1 tablespoon lemon juice salt to taste ground black pepper to taste DIRECTIONS: 1. Combine tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions in a salad bowl. Season to taste with salt and black pepper. Sprinkle with lemon juice. Chill.
