Chutney</h3></div><div class="non_tab"><br><img src="http://images.meredith.com//betterrecipes/images/tmp/shell/FiveStarsIconWhite.gif" class="star"></div></div><div class="bottom"><p class="description">Baked chicken, topped with white cheddar cheese and a sweet apple raisin chutney.</p><br class="clearfix"><div class="hr"></div><br class="clearfix"><ul class="tools"><li><a rel="nofollow" title="Email Recipe" href="http://recipes.betterrecipes.com/betterrecipes/emailRecipe.jsp?recipeId=mem2320900058" class="email">Email Recipe</a></li><li><a rel="nofollow" title="Print Recipe" href="http://recipes.betterrecipes.com/betterrecipes/printRecipe.jsp?recipeId=mem2320900058" class="print">Print Recipe</a></li><li>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</li></ul><h4>Ingredients:</h4><ul class="items"><li>2 Tablespoons extra virgin olive oil</li><li>8 boneless skinless chicken breasts</li><li>1 teaspoon onion powder</li><li>1 teaspoon garlic powder</li><li>1/4 teaspoon ground allspice</li><li>Salt and pepper</li><li>8 slices white cheddar cheese</li><li>Granny Smith apples, sliced, for garnish</li><li>Mint sprigs, for garnish</li><li></li><li>Apple Raisin Chutney:</li><li>2 large Granny Smith apples, peeled cored and finely chopped</li><li>1/2 cup golden raisins</li><li>1/2 cup sweet onion, finely minced</li><li>1/4 cup apple cider vinegar</li><li>2 Tablespoons water</li><li>2 Tablespoons white sugar</li><li>1 Tablespoon brown sugar</li><li>1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon</li><li>1/4 teaspoon ground allspice</li><li>1/4 teaspoon ground cloves</li></ul><h4>Method</h4><p class="black">Preheat oven to 400 degrees.Add olive oil to a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken with onion powder, garlic powder, allspice, salt and pepper.  Add chicken to oil and cook about 3 minutes per side then remove from heat.Transfer chicken to a large, rimmed baking sheet.  Top each chicken breast with a slice of white cheddar cheese. Place baking sheet in oven and bake for 5-7 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and remove from oven.To serve, place two White Cheddar Chicken breasts on each of 4 plates.  Place a little bit of Apple Raisin Chutney on top of each breast and a more generous helping of Apple Raisin Chutney on the side of each plate.  Garnish with apple slices and mint.Makes 4 servings.Apple-Raisin Chutney:Combine apples, raisins, onion, 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar, water, sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, allspice, ground cloves in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat.  Stir to dissolve sugar, bring to a boil then reduce heat and cover.  Simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until liquid has cooked off. Season to taste with salt.<br><br>								Notes:								</p><h4>Number of servings: <span class="black">4</span></h4><p class="orange">              								Submitted By: film_score<br><a
