with  Quick B&eacute;arnaise Sauce</FONT></U></B></CENTER></P>  <DL>    <DT><FONT FACE="Arial">1/2 cup mayonnaise <BR>    1 tablespoon minced shallots <BR>    2 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon OR 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon,    crushed<BR>    1 1/2 teaspoons tarragon or red wine vinegar <BR>    1/8 teaspoon freshly ground pepper</FONT>    <DT><FONT FACE="Arial">16 jumbo shrimp, peeled, deveined, with    the tail segment left on<BR>    8 strips smoked bacon, cut crosswise in half <BR>    16 wooden toothpicks, soaked in water for 20 minutes and drained</FONT>  </DL>  <OL>    <LI><FONT FACE="Arial">For the b&eacute;arnaise sauce: Combine    the mayonnaise, shallots, tarragon, vinegar and pepper in a small    bowl. Cover and let stand at room temperature to blend the flavors,    at least 1 hour.</FONT>    <LI><FONT FACE="Arial">Wrap a bacon strip around each shrimp,    securing the bacon with a toothpick.</FONT>    <LI><FONT COLOR="#000000" FACE="Arial">Prepare barbecue (medium-high    heat). Brush grill rack lightly with oil.</FONT>    <LI><FONT FACE="Arial">Grill, covered, turning once, until the    bacon is crisp and the shrimp is firm, about 8 minute.</FONT>    <LI><FONT FACE="Arial">Remove toothpicks and serve immediately    with the sauce.</FONT>  </OL>  <P><FONT FACE="Arial">Makes 16 appetizers.</FONT></P></BLOCKQUOTE><P><CENTER><B><!--NOEDIT--></div><!--/NOEDIT--></B></CENTER></P><BLOCKQUOTE>  <P><TABLE WIDTH="800" BORDER="0" CELLSPACING="2" CELLPADDING="5">    <TR>
